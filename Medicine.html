Medicine List

Medicine Name Generic Name / Brand Name Dosage Considerations when taking
Paracetamol Acetaminophen / Tylenol 500mg every 4-6 hours Avoid alcohol, don't exceed 4g/day. Can cause liver damage.
Ibuprofen Advil / Motrin 200-400mg every 4-6 hours Take with food to avoid stomach irritation. Do not exceed 1200mg per day without doctor's advice.
Amoxicillin Amoxil / Trimox 250-500mg every 8 hours Complete the full course, even if you feel better. May cause gastrointestinal upset.
Aspirin Acetylsalicylic Acid / Bayer 81-325mg daily Not recommended for children (can cause Reye's syndrome). Take with food to avoid stomach irritation.
